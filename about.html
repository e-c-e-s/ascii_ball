What is ASCII PONG?

This is my take on an iconic 'Atari PONG', which came out couple years ago, in 1972 to be precise. By the way, if you haven't played 'Atari PONG' yet, be sure to check it out, should still be on sale in some of the stores. A decade earlier, in 1963 the American Standard Association's X3.2 subcommittee (cool word btw) has developed this character encoding standard called American Standard Code for Information Interchange, or ASCII for short. So why is my game called 'ASCII PONG'? Mainly because it uses ASCII encoding to give you a beautiful visual representation of the ping-pong game on the screen of your microprocessor-based computing machine!
